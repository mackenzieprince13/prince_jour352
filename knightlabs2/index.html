Union Beach Strong: How SuperStorm Sandy Destroyed Small Jersey Shore Town

In 2012, a devastating superstorm struck along the East Coast, destroying miles of beaches and property. One of the areas affected was a one square mile town at the top of Jersey Shore: Union Beach. Despite their lack of media appeal in the past, housing only 5,500 residents, Union Beach garnered plenty of coverage, due to their intense flooding and housing damages.
